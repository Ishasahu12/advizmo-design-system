Recommendation

Recommended Concept

Concept 1 (Cash Flow Summary) is recommended as the primary design. It balances scanability, financial clarity, and premium feel while supporting complete cash flow.

Why Concept 1 Wins

  • ✓ Shows complete cash flow (expenses + income)
  • ✓ Financial impact visible at a glance
  • ✓ Fast to scan — users understand in 2-3 seconds
  • ✓ Premium feel without being conversational
  • ✓ Scales well with more or fewer changes

When to Use Each Concept

Cash Flow Summary
Default choice. Best for most users. Balanced approach.
Review Summary
When many changes exist. Summarize, don't overwhelm.
Priority Updates
When one change is significantly larger. Focus attention.
Grouped Review
When both income and expenses change. Clear separation.
Financial Assistant
For premium users who prefer conversational guidance. Highest trust feel.

Writing Principles

✓ Do
  • • Use "cash flow" not "recurring bills"
  • • Show numbers, not just descriptions
  • • Lead with what changed
  • • Keep descriptions under 2 lines
✗ Don't
  • • Use "subscriptions" alone
  • • Explain the product
  • • Use internal terminology
  • • Write more than 2 lines
