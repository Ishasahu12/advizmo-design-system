Recommendation

Concept 3: Priority Stack

For a fintech app like Advizmo, Concept 3 (Priority Stack) is the recommended approach. It balances visual hierarchy, user motivation, and Apple HIG compliance while driving the most important action.

  • Progress psychology: The 60% completion bar motivates users to close the gap, leveraging the Zeigarnik effect for incomplete tasks.
  • Clear primary action: The large card with CTA button makes the #1 priority unmistakable, reducing decision fatigue.
  • Scannable secondary items: Compact list format allows users to see other options without overwhelming them.
  • iOS native patterns: Follows Apple HIG with card-based layouts and standard navigation patterns.
  • Conversion focused: Directs attention to the highest-value action (account connection) which unlocks better recommendations.

Winner Concept 3: Priority Stack

Best balance of motivation, clarity, and conversion. Progress bar drives completion while clear hierarchy guides users to the most impactful action first.

Runner-up Concept 5: Guided Flow

Excellent for users who prefer step-by-step guidance. However, the sequential nature may feel restrictive for users who want to choose their own path.

Honorable Concept 4: Contextual

Strong AI-driven approach that explains "why" each action matters. Best for power users who want transparency, but adds cognitive load for casual users.

Alternative Concept 1: Clean List

Simplest approach with lowest cognitive load. Good fallback option when you want to present all options equally without bias toward any single action.
