Writing Style Guide

Benefit-driven UX writing. Answer: What can I do? Why does it matter? What happens next?

Feature-centric (avoid)
"Connect your bank account using Plaid integration for read-only access"
Benefit-driven (use)
"See your full financial picture in one place"
Feature-centric (avoid)
"Enable auto-investment with configurable thresholds"
Benefit-driven (use)
"Put idle cash to work — no effort required"
Feature-centric (avoid)
"Sync transaction data for cash flow analysis"
Benefit-driven (use)
"Know exactly where your money goes each month"
Feature-centric (avoid)
"Add recurring income entries for balance projections"
Benefit-driven (use)
"Track your income to find more to invest"
Feature-centric (avoid)
"Bank-level 256-bit AES encryption with SOC 2 certification"
Benefit-driven (use)
"Your money is protected by the same security banks use"
Feature-centric (avoid)
"Portfolio diversification across asset classes"
Benefit-driven (use)
"Grow your money while keeping risk low"

Writing Principles

Apple HIG
1
Lead with the benefit
Tell users what they will gain, not how it works.
2
Use active voice
"Connect your account" not "Your account can be connected."
3
Short sentences
15 words or fewer. One idea per sentence.
4
Avoid banking jargon
"Money" not "capital." "Save" not "accumulate."
5
Create urgency
"Start earning today" not "You may want to consider investing."
6
Answer the next question
After reading, users should know exactly what to do next.

Key Metrics to Track

Measure success at each stage of the disconnected-to-invested journey.

Time to First Value

Seconds until user understands the app purpose. Target: less than 10s.

Connection Start Rate

% of users who tap Connect Account. Target: greater than 40%.

✅

Connection Completion

% who finish connecting after starting. Target: greater than 65%.

⏱

Time to First Investment

Days from signup to first investment. Target: less than 3 days.

📊

Action Row Engagement

% of users who interact with suggested actions. Target: greater than 25%.

💰

Average Idle Cash Invested

$ amount invested per user. Target: greater than $3,000.
