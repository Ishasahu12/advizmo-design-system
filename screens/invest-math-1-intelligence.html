Why we rebalance
Keeping your recipe on track

Over time, some investments grow faster than others. Your 58% US Stocks might drift to 62%. That's too much risk.

1
Drift detected — US Stocks moved from 58% to 62%
2
Rebalance triggers — We sell a bit of US Stocks, buy more International and Bonds
3
Back to target — Portfolio returns to your ideal mix

This keeps your risk level consistent and often improves long-term returns.
