Semantic Surface System

How recommendation cards use extremely subtle gradients to communicate intent rather than decoration. Each surface conveys meaning through color without competing with content.

Foundation
Gradient Rules
The principles governing semantic surface usage

Design Principles

1
Gradients extremely subtle — background mostly white
2
Only provide semantic guidance, never decoration
3
Never reduce readability of text content
4
Never compete with the card's content or CTA
5
Generous whitespace — let the surface breathe
6
Premium, calm, trustworthy feel throughout
7
If meaning doesn't change, don't change gradient
